Welcome to Wildlands of Faenora

Embark on an epic journey through the enchanting Wildlands of Faenora, where legends are born and heroes are forged. Inspired by classic tactical RPGs, Wildlands of Faenora combines strategic squad-based combat with rogue-lite elements, set in a mesmerizing world of magic and mystery.

Key Features

Tactical Squad Battles

Lead your squad of unique units in thrilling turn-based combat. Position your heroes wisely on a 3x3 grid and outsmart your enemies with your tactical brilliance.

Unit Customization

Equip your units with powerful weapons, armor, and accessories. Promote them to advanced classes to unlock new abilities and dominate the battlefield.

Dynamic Path Choices

Every decision matters as you navigate the treacherous Wildlands. Choose your path wisely, encountering various events, battles, and treasures along the way.

Strategic Planning

Optimize your units' strengths and unique skills (and your likelihood of survival) through strategic grid placement. Melee fighters get extra attacks in the front rows and ranged units excel in the rear.

Pixel Art Aesthetic

Immerse yourself in a visually captivating experience with retro-inspired pixel art graphics that bring the world of Faenora to life.

The Story

In the heart of Faenora, a realm where magic and might intertwine, your hero rises to forge their name as a Faenoran legend. Drawn by tales of a fearsome Gryphon, you, the hero, embark on a quest for glory and adventure. Armed with an enchanted Phoenix Amulet that protects you from the lasting embrace of death, you set out with a band of loyal companions and venture into the untamed Wildlands to hunt the Gryphon. What other secrets might you uncover along the way? The fate of Faenora lies in your hands.
